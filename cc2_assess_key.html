The length of a rectangular field is 7 meters less than four times its width. The perimeter is 136 meters. Calculate the length and the width.

1 pt. correct answer
1 pt. showing work
Answer:
Width = 15 meters
Length = 53 meters
Let width = x Length = 4x − 7 \[ 2(4x - 7) + 2x = 136 \] \[ 8x - 14 + 2x = 136 \] \[ 10x = 150 \] \[ x = 15 \] \[ 4(15) - 7 = 53 \]

1 pt. for the correct answer
1 pt. for showing work that could lead to the correct answer

Answer: 16,800 more candies
Initial per hour: \[ 75 \times 20 = 1500 \] New per hour: \[ 180 \times 20 = 3600 \] Difference per hour: \[ 3600 - 1500 = 2100 \] Over 8 hours: \[ 2100 \times 8 = 16,800 \]
